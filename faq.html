FAQs

Studio Location?

Lubnag Photography does not own a studio. If a studio is desired, Client is responsible for the additional payment of the studio separately. If Client would like Madelyn to rent the studio, there will be additional fees. If Client is renting the studio, Client is responsible for requesting set-up materials from studio management. Any addition materials and props desired will be the Client's responsibility to provide. Madelyn can provide studio reccomendations if needed.

Payment?

50% of the total initial package cost is due at time of signing the given contract and will serve as a non-refundable retainer fee. Balance is payable in full is due the day of the session date. The session photos will not be processed or delivered unless payment is recieved in full. Madelyn accepts cash and electionic payments via Apple Pay and Venmo.

Cancellation or Reschedule?

The security deposit paid at the signing of the contract secures the date and time of the session and is non-refundable. If the CLient cencels the session, the remaining charges will not be owed. In the event of a need of reschedule: Madelyn allows 1 reschedule within 6 months after the original session date. In the event that Client does not reschedule within the allotted time frame, the security deposit will be forfeited and cannot be used for any following sessions.

Raw Images?

Madelyn does not provide raw images in any of her photography packages. Raw images can be provided to Client with an additional $100 fee.

Duration of Time?

Photo editing and processing will take approximately 2-4 weeks after the session has been captured. Photos will be delivered digitally via a cloud storage. Expedited services will be an additional fee depending on the photography package and timeline ranging from $75+.
